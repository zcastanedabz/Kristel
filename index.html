Mission

Escuela Secundaria Tecnica Mexico will provide education, promote knowledge, and develop skills and positive attitudes in the vocational/technical fields through theoretical and practical application of learning to prepare productive citizens in society.

Vision

Escuela Secundaria Tecnica Mexico strives to become an educational community integrating learning to produce knowledge, highly skilled, and responsible citizens for a productive, cooperative, progressive, and self-sustainable society.
